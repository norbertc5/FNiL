Der Kiefergezahnt is a creature created by the laboratory. He's very strong. Characteristic feature of his apperance is a heavily modified jaw.

The scientists worked on creating it for almost a year on the orders of an anonymous client. To increase the creature's strength, the scientists gave it a lot of medication and it underwent a long hormone therapy. They also performed several operations to modify its jaws.

The creature's behavior is unpredictable and he is very aggressive. He has to be on sedatives in order for the scientists to work with him.
